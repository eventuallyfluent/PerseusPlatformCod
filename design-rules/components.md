# Component Rules

## Cards

- Cards are the primary unit of the storefront and learner portal.
- Use dark panels, subtle borders, compact metadata, and a controlled hover lift.
- Rich cards are allowed on sales/homepage surfaces.
- Catalog cards must be compact enough for comparison browsing.

## Course Cards

- Course cards should be image-led when an image exists.
- Use a compact metadata row: instructor/source plus one meaningful status badge.
- Show title, short supporting copy, price, and one clear action.
- Do not reserve large blank space just to align cards.
- Do not use meaningless labels such as `Included` when the user needs `Free preview`, `Members only`, `Locked`, `Current`, or price/action context.

## Badges

- Badges are uppercase, pill-shaped, and semantic.
- Premium uses gold.
- Free uses muted or success styling depending on context.
- Current/completed/paid/locked/pending/refunded use status colors with actual meaning.

## Collections

- Collections on catalog pages are navigation aids.
- Keep them compact: title, course count, active state, and a short cue.
- Homepage collection panels may remain richer and more editorial.

## Footer

- Preserve the original four-column footer structure:
  - brand
  - platform
  - legal
  - email opt-in
